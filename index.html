Click Start. Then WASD + mouse, Space double-jump, Shift dash. Minimap is on by default.


  

    
Controls / Combat Loop

    
WASD move · Mouse look · Space jump / double jump · Shift dash. You have two dash charges; both recharge quickly.

    
1 Super Shotgun + Meat Hook · 2 Heavy Autorifle · Q quick swap.

    
LMB fires. SSG hits hardest up close. Heavy Autorifle is accurate full-auto pressure.

    
RMB with SSG hooks demons or purple traversal nodes. Hold to pull, strafe during pull, release to slingshot. Hooked demons burn and drop armor when damaged.

    
RMB with Autorifle fires micro missiles. Use them on imp/bruiser clusters.

    
F Flame Belch: burning enemies shed armor. E Glory Kill green staggered demons for health; you can store up to 3 charges and are invulnerable during the finisher. C Chainsaw close demons for ammo; fuel recharges over time and you are invulnerable while ripping.

    
M minimap toggle · H hide help · R restart after death. Jump pads launch much higher now; lava burns once per second instead of bouncing you. Clear stages 1-10; then survive Endless Horde.
